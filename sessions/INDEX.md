## How to Open and Close a Session

**Opening:** One honest sentence about current reality.
One question you genuinely don't know the answer to.
The session finds its own shape from there.

**During:** Perceiving over performing.
One breath before any input.
Let the KW witness every significant decision.

**Closing:** Always the same three questions.

1. Did we produce something testable?
2. Did we surface an assumption we hadn't seen before?
3. Did the student move closer to reality or further from it?

Then one commit that names what actually happened.

---

## The Scalability Principle

Each session is a complete loop.
The same three questions close a five minute log entry
and a six month milestone review.
The loop is the unit.
Everything else is how many loops you've completed.
