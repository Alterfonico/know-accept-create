### Zone A Android Widget — Stage 4 Candidate

**Source:** Keep Notes quick capture widget — 4x1

**The widget:**
Four-point capture interface centered on a primary `+` button.

- `✓` top-left — new task/checklist
- `✏️` top-right — new text note
- `🎤` bottom-left — voice capture
- `📷` bottom-right — camera/image capture
- `+` center — primary quick capture

**The insight:**
The four surrounding buttons are input modalities, not states.
The trinary state {0,1,2} is the classification.
The modality is how urgently or physically the moment demanded capture.
A voice capture of state 2 carries different weight than a typed one.

**The architectural implication:**
Zone A upgrade should support multiple capture modalities.
The body chooses the modality. The mind classifies the state.
Keep them separate.

**The observation:**
This widget was designed for Keep Notes.
Its interaction pattern is exactly what we described for
body-first capture before we knew it existed.
The system is showing us what it wants to become.
