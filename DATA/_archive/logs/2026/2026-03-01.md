# 2026-03-01

## Trigger

Insight loss between emergence and capture.

## Context

Reviewing a lifetime of failed capture tools — paper, analog, digital note-taking apps.
All failed at the same point: the friction between the insight arriving and the hand
reaching for something to record it. By then the thread is already dissolving.

## Observation

The failure is not the tool. The failure is the gap between the body knowing
and the hand acting. Every existing solution asks the mind to interrupt itself
to record itself. That interruption is the loss.

## The Dream

A body-first capture layer. A smartwatch tap pattern or gesture that meets
the insight at the moment it arrives — before the mind has time to lose it.
Raw capture first. Elaboration later, in a low-friction review session.
